INTERNATIONAL EVENTS

FAMILY GATHERING

Family gathering is an activity gather all generations of dual degree majors, both alumni and new students. In this event, each generation is obliged to introduce themselves to each other so they can get to know each generation. Not only that, gathering event also contains games aim to strengthen brotherhood between generations and build good relationships with each other as part of International community.

STUDENT EXCHANGE

Student exchange activities are dual degree students visiting HELP University. This activity is held annually and usually 3rd year students visit. In this student exchange activity students are given knowledge about team building, introduction about HELP Campus, and relate to friends from HELP University.
